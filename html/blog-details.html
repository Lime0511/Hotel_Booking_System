5 Must-Visit Attractions Near Johor Bahru

  • 10th July, 2025
  • Newton Singh

Planning a trip to Johor Bahru? Whether you're a first-time visitor or returning for more, Johor offers a vibrant blend of culture, nature, and fun. Here are five attractions near Indah Hotel that you simply can't miss during your stay!

1. LEGOLAND Malaysia Resort

This world-renowned theme park is a must-visit for families and LEGO fans. Featuring over 40 rides, shows, and attractions—including a water park and SEA LIFE aquarium—LEGOLAND promises a full day of excitement. It’s just 30 minutes from Johor Bahru city center.

2. Johor Premium Outlets (JPO)

If shopping is on your agenda, head over to Johor Premium Outlets in Kulai. This open-air shopping haven features global brands like Coach, Nike, and Michael Kors at discounted prices. A great place to snag deals and enjoy al fresco cafes while you shop.

3. Desaru Coast

Craving a beach escape? Desaru Coast offers stunning seaside views, luxurious resorts, and water activities including surfing and banana boat rides. It's about an hour's drive away and worth it for a sunny day out.

4. Sultan Abu Bakar State Mosque

This iconic mosque stands majestically on a hill overlooking the Straits of Johor. Built in the late 19th century, it’s known for its Victorian-Moorish architecture and peaceful ambiance. A must for history and culture lovers.

5. Pasar Karat (JB Night Market)

End your day with a trip to Pasar Karat, Johor Bahru’s famous night market. From vintage goods and street food to local crafts, this lively market is perfect for an evening stroll and souvenir hunt.
